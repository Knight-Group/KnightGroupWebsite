Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Knight Group serve?

We serve all of Pinellas County, FL — including Safety Harbor, Clearwater, Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Largo, Tarpon Springs, Oldsmar, and surrounding communities.

Is Knight Group licensed and insured?

Yes. Knight Group Handyman Services LLC is fully registered and insured in Florida, giving you complete peace of mind on every job.

Does Knight Group offer free estimates?

Yes — we provide free written estimates for all projects. Call (813) 649-3341 or use our online form to request yours today.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

We typically respond within 24 hours for standard requests. For urgent situations, we offer 24/7 emergency services — call any time.

What handyman services do you provide?

We provide general repairs, plumbing, electrical work, carpentry & framing, painting, home renovations, door & window installation, custom projects, and 24/7 emergency services throughout Pinellas County.

Do you charge a minimum service fee?

No — unlike many competitors, Knight Group does not charge a two-hour minimum. You pay only for the time your job actually takes, making us one of the most cost-effective choices in Pinellas County.
