Safe, professional electrical services for your home. Minor electrical repairs and fixture installations using existing connections.

Professional Electrical Services

Knight Group provides safe, reliable electrical services throughout Pinellas County. Our team handles minor electrical repairs, fixture installations, and replacements using existing electrical connections with strict adherence to safety standards.

⚠️ Safety First

All electrical work is performed by experienced technicians following local codes and safety standards. We prioritize your safety and the integrity of your electrical systems.

Our Electrical Services Include:

  • Outlet installation and repair (like-for-like replacement)
  • Light fixture mounting and installation
  • Ceiling fan installation and repair
  • Switch installation and replacement (like-for-like)
  • GFCI outlet installation (replacement only)
  • Minor electrical repairs
  • Electrical troubleshooting and diagnostics
  • Cord and plug repairs
  • Electrical safety inspections

Common Electrical Projects

Outlet Installation: Replacing existing outlets with new ones, USB outlets, and GFCI outlet replacements for bathroom and kitchen safety.

Lighting Solutions: Installing new light fixtures using existing wiring, chandeliers, and pendant lights.

Ceiling Fans: Professional installation using existing electrical connections with proper mounting and balancing.

Electrical Repairs & Troubleshooting

We diagnose and repair electrical issues including:

  • Flickering lights (fixture-related issues)
  • Dead outlets (when not circuit-related)
  • Faulty switches and dimmers
  • Loose connections
  • Damaged plugs and cords
  • Light fixture problems

Safety & Compliance Notice

Important: For safety and legal compliance, we do not perform electrical panel work, circuit breaker installation, new wiring runs, or work requiring electrical permits. For these services, we can refer you to a professional electrician. All our work is performed safely using existing electrical connections.

Frequently Asked Questions

What electrical work can a handyman legally do in Florida?

Florida handymen can perform minor electrical tasks such as replacing outlets, switches, light fixtures, ceiling fans, and doorbell wiring without a permit in most cases. Work requiring a permit is coordinated with a licensed electrician.

Can you install ceiling fans and light fixtures?

Yes — ceiling fan installation and light fixture swaps are two of our most requested electrical services throughout Pinellas County and Safety Harbor.

Do you replace GFCI outlets?

Yes. GFCI outlet replacement is a standard service we perform in kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor areas throughout Pinellas County.

What happens if I need a full rewire?

If a job requires a full electrical permit or a licensed contractor, we'll let you know upfront and can refer you to a trusted licensed electrician in the area.
