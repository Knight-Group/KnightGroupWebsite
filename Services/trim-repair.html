Trim profiles and damage types we see

Trim is the visual frame of every room — chips and gaps read louder than wall scuffs.

  • Baseboard dents from vacuums and furniture moves
  • Casing splits at latch height on bedroom doors
  • Missing shoe molding after flooring upgrades
  • Chair rail gaps when walls settle slightly
  • Garage entry trim swollen from moisture
  • Window stool and apron repairs after sill leaks
  • Crown sections loosened by attic ladder impacts
  • Paint-grade MDF repairs in closets and laundry rooms

Installation quality at transitions

We cope inside corners when profiles allow, scarf long runs at hidden locations, and sand profiles smooth before enamel. Caulk joints are tooled for water-shedding profiles in baths and laundry spaces.
