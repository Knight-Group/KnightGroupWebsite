Door behavior we correct

Doors are dynamic — seasonal movement, carpet changes, and hinge wear alter clearances month to month.

  • Planing binding edges on hollow-core slabs
  • Shimming hinges when frames lean slightly
  • Adjusting continuous hinges on heavy exterior units
  • Lowering astragals on double doors that drag
  • Resetting pocket door tracks and guide pins
  • Tuning self-closing hinges on garage entries
  • Parallel gaps around slabs for even reveal lines
  • Silencing squeaks with pin swaps and lubricants rated for interiors

Minimizing disruption

Doors stay on hinges when possible — we mark planed edges for future reference and vacuum dust at the work area. Paint touch-ups can be bundled when edges are raw wood.

Door adjustment and hinge repair in place

Doors that drag on carpet, swing open on their own, or rub at the header rarely need full replacement. Knight Group plans edges, adjusts hinges, swaps bent pins, and shims frames for smooth travel — everyday door adjustment handyman service.

Adjustment scenarios

  • Seasonal swelling on bath and exterior doors
  • Slab doors that bind after new flooring thickness
  • Self-closing hinges on garage-to-house fire doors (scope on site)
  • Latch misses cured with strike moves or hinge pad adjustments
  • Pocket and bypass closet doors that jump tracks

Minimize dust and finish damage

We mark planed edges, use sharp planes or sanders with vac attachment, and touch up paint when necessary. Pair with trim painting when scuffs are widespread.
