Interior, Exterior & Drywall Finishing Services

Knight Group Handyman Services provides comprehensive painting and finishing solutions for residential and commercial properties throughout Pinellas County. Our skilled painters deliver exceptional results with meticulous attention to detail. Please note: Customers supply all paints and materials.

Our Painting Services Include:

  • Interior painting (walls, ceilings, trim)
  • Exterior painting and staining
  • Cabinet painting and refinishing
  • Drywall repair and finishing
  • Texture matching and application
  • Primer application and surface preparation
  • Wallpaper removal and installation
  • Deck and fence staining
  • Specialty finishes and techniques
  • Touch-up and maintenance painting
  • Color consultation and matching

Popular Painting Projects in Pinellas County Homes

  • Single-room repaints and rental turnover refreshes
  • Drywall patch + texture match + paint blend work
  • Trim, baseboard, door, and cabinet repainting
  • Exterior touch-ups around doors, soffits, fascia, and siding
  • Fence, deck, and entryway stain refreshes

When this page is the best route

Start with Painting & Finishing when the job depends on prep quality, texture matching, or a clean finish in visible living spaces. This page fits occupied homes, single-room refreshes, turn-ready rentals, and repair work that needs to blend into surrounding walls and trim.

Why Choose Knight Group for Painting?

With over 15 years of experience, we understand that quality painting is an investment in your property. That's why we provide:

  • Thorough surface preparation
  • Clean, professional workmanship
  • Detailed color consultation
  • Warranty on all work performed

Painting Process & Quality

Our professional painting process ensures long-lasting, beautiful results:

Surface Preparation: Proper cleaning, patching, and priming for optimal paint adhesion.

Paints & Materials: All paints and materials are supplied by the customer. We use professional techniques to ensure the best results with your chosen products.

Expert Application: Professional techniques for smooth, even coverage and crisp, clean lines.

Interior Painting Excellence

Transform your living spaces with our professional interior painting services. We handle everything from single rooms to whole-house painting projects, always using professional techniques to achieve a flawless finish with your supplied paints.

Exterior Painting & Protection

Protect and beautify your home's exterior with our weather-resistant painting solutions. We apply your chosen exterior paints designed to withstand Florida's climate while maintaining their beauty for years to come.

What changes a painting estimate

Most painting quotes depend on surface condition, prep time, ceiling height, trim detail, occupancy, and whether drywall repair or texture matching is needed before paint can go on. We break those items out clearly so you know whether the price is coming from prep, finish work, or the total scope.

Route related work to the right page

If the job starts with wall damage, leaks, or patching before paint, review General Repairs. If the project expands into flooring, tile, or a wider room refresh, use Home Renovations. You can also review recent proof on the gallery, confirm city coverage on service areas, or send the full request through booking.

Where we paint most often

We regularly handle painting projects in Safety Harbor, Clearwater, Dunedin, Largo, Palm Harbor, Pinellas Park, and nearby Pinellas neighborhoods, from occupied homes that need careful room-by-room work to rentals that need fast turnover painting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do interior and exterior painting?

Yes — we offer both interior and exterior painting services throughout Pinellas County, including wall prep, priming, texture matching, and finish coat application.

Can you match my existing wall texture?

Yes. Texture matching (orange peel, knockdown, smooth) is part of our standard drywall and painting service, so repairs blend seamlessly.

Do you paint just one room?

Absolutely. There is no minimum size requirement — we take on single-room paint jobs, accent walls, and even just trim and baseboards.

What paint brands and finishes do you use?

We work with industry-standard paints including Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore and can help you choose the right sheen for each surface.
