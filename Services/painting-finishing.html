Interior and exterior painting in Pinellas County

Paint is often the fastest way to make a repair disappear — but only if prep is done right. Knight Group handles room refreshes, trim painting, and touch-ups across Safety Harbor and Pinellas County with the same attention to punch-list detail as our repair work.

Room painting, trim, and touch-ups

  • Interior walls and ceilings after drywall or plaster repair
  • Trim, doors, and cabinet touch-ups (not full spray-shop refinishing)
  • Exterior fascia, soffit, and spot painting where prep allows
  • Caulk, fill, sand, prime, and paint sequences that last in Florida humidity

Prep work that makes paint last

We patch holes, match texture where feasible, sand glossy surfaces, and prime stains before topcoat. Homeowners comparing quotes should ask what prep is included — that is where cheap paint jobs fail in coastal humidity.
