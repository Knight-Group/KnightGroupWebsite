Door frame repair for split jambs and bad strikes

Kicks, humidity, and repeated slamming split door frames and loosen strikes. Knight Group repairs jambs, re-anchors hinges, fills gouges, and aligns latches so doors close securely — core door frame repair work in Pinellas County homes and rentals.

Frame damage we correct

  • Split side jambs at hinge and strike locations
  • Loose frames pulled back to rough opening with long screws or shims
  • Strike plate relocation when latches miss due to settling
  • Threshold and transition issues at exterior entries (scope on site)
  • Paint-ready fills when patches are cosmetic

Security and weather sealing

A door that will not latch is both an comfort and security issue. We verify alignment, replace short screws with structural fasteners where needed, and recommend weatherstripping when daylight shows at the perimeter.

Frequently asked questions

Common questions homeowners ask before booking this type of work in Pinellas County.

Can you fix a split door jamb?

Yes — localized splits at hinges or strikes are common door frame repairs.

Do you move strike plates for better latch alignment?

Yes — when frames have settled slightly and latches miss.

Can exterior door frames be repaired?

Minor repairs and alignment yes — severely rotted exterior frames may need replacement scopes quoted separately.

Will frame repair include paint touch-up?

Paint-ready fills are included; full painting can be bundled if needed.

Do you repair frames after break-in damage?

Cosmetic and alignment repairs may be possible — structural security upgrades are assessed on site.

Can you fix a door that will not lock?

Often yes when latch and strike alignment are the root cause.
