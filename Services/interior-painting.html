Prep determines longevity

We sand glossy areas, degloss where needed, caulk gaps, and prime before topcoat. Cheap paint over unprepared patches fails first at shower walls and south-facing rooms — we call that out in estimates.

Interior painting in occupied Pinellas homes

Open floor plans in Westchase and Carrollwood-style layouts need consistent sheen from kitchen to hall — we cut in carefully at tray ceilings visible from entry sightlines.

Older Clearwater bungalows with plaster overlays get compatibility checks before heavy sanding — we adapt prep to substrate instead of forcing one system everywhere.

Interior painting estimates

Room dimensions, ceiling height, and current color help us estimate prep time. Flag occupied nurseries or pet areas so we plan low-odor products when needed.
