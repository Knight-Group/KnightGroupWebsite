Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of custom projects do you take on?

We build custom shelving, entertainment centers, closet systems, Murphy beds, outdoor storage, window seats, and other specialty woodworking or handyman projects tailored to your space.

Can you work from a design I found online?

Absolutely. Bring us a photo, sketch, or Pinterest reference and we will work with you to adapt it to your specific space and budget.

Do you handle both indoor and outdoor custom work?

Yes — we build custom features for interiors (built-ins, storage, trim accents) and exteriors (pergolas, benches, planters, storage sheds) across Pinellas County.

How do I get a quote for a custom project?

Call (813) 649-3341 or submit the contact form for a free consultation. We come to your home, measure the space, and provide a written estimate.
