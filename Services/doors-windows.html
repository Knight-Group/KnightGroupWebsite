FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Common questions homeowners ask before booking this type of work in Pinellas County.

Can you install new exterior doors?

Yes. We install pre-hung exterior doors, security doors, screen doors, and sliding glass doors throughout Pinellas County. We ensure proper weather sealing and alignment.

Do you repair or replace windows?

Yes — we handle window glass replacement, frame repair, weatherstripping, and full window unit swaps for standard residential windows.

Why is my door sticking or not closing properly?

Florida's humidity causes wood frames to swell, hinges to loosen, and strike plates to shift. We diagnose and fix the root cause rather than just planing the door down.

Can you install a storm door?

Yes. Storm door installation is one of our most popular services in Pinellas County — we install and adjust for a weathertight fit.
