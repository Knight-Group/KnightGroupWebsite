Custom Handcrafted Wooden Furniture & Artistic Resins

Handcrafted Creations Tailored for Your Space

At Knight Group, we specialize in crafting bespoke, one-of-a-kind wooden furniture that brings personality and elegance to any space. Using rare and exotic woods, hand-selected for quality and beauty, each piece we create is meticulously crafted to ensure it’s truly unique.

Alongside our fine woodworking, we offer custom artistic resin designs. With our epoxy resin techniques, we can integrate natural elements—like stones or wood fragments—or themed pieces into the furniture, creating a statement that resonates with your personal style. Imagine a coffee table encasing cherished items or even miniature scenes, transforming a functional piece into a captivating work of art.

We welcome custom orders, working closely with clients to bring their vision to life, whether its a rustic reclaimed wood dining table or a modern piece with intricate resin detailing. At Knight Group, our commitment is to design furniture that not only enhances your space but also endures for years to come. Reach out to discuss your ideas, and lets create something exceptional together.
