Custom Handcrafted Wooden Furniture & Artistic Resins

Handcrafted Creations for Your Space

At Knight Group, we take pride in producing bespoke, handcrafted wooden furniture that transforms any space into something unique and personal. We specialize in using rare and exotic woods, carefully selected for their quality and beauty. Each piece is meticulously crafted by hand, ensuring that no two items are exactly alike.

In addition to fine woodworking, we also offer artistic resin work. Our epoxy resin techniques allow us to encapsulate scenes or items into the furniture itself. Picture a beautifully crafted coffee table with intricate resin work, perhaps encasing natural elements like stones and wood fragments, or themed objects like figurines from a favorite collection. The possibilities are endless, making our furniture both a functional piece of art and a conversation starter in your home or office.

Whether you're looking for a rustic dining table made from reclaimed wood or a modern piece that seamlessly integrates resin designs, our furniture is made to order and tailored specifically to your preferences. Our goal is to create furniture that is not only visually stunning but also durable enough to stand the test of time.
