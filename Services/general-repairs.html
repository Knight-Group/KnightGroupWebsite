Complete General Repair Services

Knight Group handles day-to-day repair work for homeowners and small properties across Safety Harbor and Pinellas County, from minor fixes that keep piling up to larger repair punch lists that need one dependable point of contact.

Our General Repair Services Include:

  • Leak detection and repair (pipes, faucets, fixtures)
  • Drywall repair and patching
  • Hole repair in walls and ceilings
  • Cabinet door and drawer repair
  • Window and door adjustments
  • Squeaky floor and stair repairs
  • Loose railing and banister fixes
  • Caulking and weatherproofing
  • Minor roof and gutter repairs
  • Appliance mounting and installation
  • General maintenance and upkeep

Typical repair calls in older Pinellas homes

  • Leak-stained drywall, trim swelling, and soft spots after plumbing or roof issues
  • Door latches, strike plates, and hardware that shift with humidity and settling
  • Caulk, weatherproofing, and finish repairs around tubs, sinks, and exterior openings
  • Patch-and-blend corrections before a paint refresh or rental turnover
  • Loose shelves, damaged cabinets, and small wear-and-tear corrections that stack up over time

What this page is best for

Use General Repairs when the work is mostly correction, patching, stabilization, or damage cleanup. This page is the best route for homeowners who need a practical repair plan before the job turns into a larger finish, paint, or replacement project.

What usually changes a repair estimate

Quotes typically change based on water damage, material replacement, access to the damaged area, whether matching finish work is needed afterward, and whether the repair stays in one room or spreads across multiple areas of the property.

Route related jobs to the right page

If the issue is mostly fixture, shutoff, drain, or leak-related plumbing work, start with Plumbing Services. If the repair also needs wall prep, texture matching, and repainting, pair it with Painting & Finishing. For active damage or urgent calls, use Emergency Services. If you already know the full scope, send the complete request through booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of general repairs do you handle?

We handle a wide range of home repairs including drywall and sheetrock repair, patching holes, fixing leaks, caulking, door hardware, cabinet adjustments, and general household maintenance throughout Pinellas County.

Can you repair water-damaged drywall?

Yes. We assess the moisture source first, then repair or replace the damaged drywall, texture-match, and prep it for paint — all in one visit for most jobs.

Do you charge a minimum service fee?

No — unlike many competitors, Knight Group does not charge a two-hour minimum. You only pay for the actual time your repair takes.

How quickly can I schedule a general repair?

Most standard repairs can be scheduled within 48 hours. For urgent situations, call (813) 649-3341 and we'll do our best to get to you same day.
