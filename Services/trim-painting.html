FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Common questions homeowners ask before booking this type of work in Pinellas County.

Do you paint baseboards without painting walls?

Yes — trim-only packages are available after repairs or when walls are already satisfactory.

Which finish do you use on trim?

Semi-gloss or satin enamel products that tolerate cleaning — sheen discussed during estimate.

Can you paint doors and casings together?

Yes — doors and casings are often bundled for consistent color and sheen.

Will you caulk trim before painting?

Yes — gap filling and nail holes are standard prep for trim painting.

How do you protect floors during trim painting?

Drop cloths and careful masking are used on occupied work areas.

Can trim painting follow water damage repair?

Yes — once surfaces are dry and repairs complete, trim can be finished to match.
