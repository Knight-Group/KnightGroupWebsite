What Qualifies as an Emergency?

Immediate Response Required:

  • Active water leaks causing property damage
  • Electrical hazards (sparks, burning smells, exposed wires)
  • Broken doors/windows compromising security
  • Storm damage requiring immediate protection
  • Sewage backups or major drain blockages
  • Heating failures in cold weather

Important: For life-threatening emergencies involving gas leaks, electrical fires, or major flooding, call 911 first, then call us for repairs.
