Room updates, flooring, tile, and finish improvements

Knight Group focuses on smaller renovation scopes that improve how a room looks, functions, and holds up over time. This page is for projects that are more involved than a one-off repair, but do not need a broad general-contractor buildout.

Our smaller renovation services include:

  • Bathroom refreshes with vanity, fixture, tile, and finish updates
  • Kitchen improvement work such as backsplash, fixture, trim, and finish packages
  • Flooring installation for luxury vinyl plank, tile, laminate, and selected hardwood areas
  • Countertop, backsplash, and trim updates that change the room without a full rebuild
  • Laundry, entry, and utility-space improvements
  • Window, door, and interior finish upgrades tied to a room refresh
  • Rebuild-and-refresh work after leaks, drywall replacement, or remediation
  • Accessibility modifications and practical layout improvements

Best fit for this page

Use Home Renovations when the project crosses into flooring, tile, trim, cabinetry, fixtures, or multiple finish trades in the same room. It is the right route for bathroom updates, kitchen refresh work, flooring replacement, and room-improvement scopes that need one coordinated estimate.

Popular renovation calls in Pinellas homes

  • Bathroom refreshes after aging fixtures, worn finishes, or moisture damage
  • Kitchen updates centered on backsplash, trim, fixtures, paint, and flooring
  • Luxury vinyl plank or tile installation tied to a wider room reset
  • Interior rebuild work after leaks, drywall replacement, or mold remediation closeout
  • Single-room upgrades that need carpentry, paint, and finish work to land cleanly

What usually changes the scope

Most renovation quotes change based on demolition needs, subfloor or wall condition, material lead time, trim detail, fixture count, and how many trades have to sequence through the same room. We break that out clearly so the estimate reflects the real room condition instead of a vague remodel allowance.

Use a more specific page when the scope narrows down

If the project is mainly wall prep, repainting, and finish restoration, use Painting & Finishing. If it is primarily repair correction, leak damage, or drywall patch work, use General Repairs. For trim, framing corrections, or built-in style work, review Carpentry & Framing. You can also compare project proof on the gallery or send the full room scope through booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

What renovation projects do you handle?

We handle bathroom updates, kitchen refreshes, flooring installation, tile work, fixture upgrades, and general interior renovation projects throughout Pinellas County.

Do you do bathroom remodels?

Yes — from simple cosmetic updates like new fixtures and tile to full bathroom refreshes. We provide a free estimate before any work begins.

Can you install new flooring?

Yes. We install luxury vinyl plank (LVP), tile, laminate, and hardwood flooring and remove old flooring as part of the project.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Most smaller renovations (one room, bathroom update, kitchen refresh) take 1—5 days. We provide a detailed timeline in your free estimate.
