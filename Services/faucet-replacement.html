  • Touch and battery-powered units when supply pressure supports them

Testing and cleanup standards

We flush lines briefly, check hot and cold orientation, inspect under-sink cabinets with a dry rag test, and haul away old fixtures when agreed. Granite and quartz decks get padded protection during wrench work.

Faucet replacement on existing supply connections

Faucet replacement handyman calls usually arrive with a box from the hardware store and a hope that nothing leaks on first use. Knight Group removes old faucets, cleans mounting surfaces, installs your new unit or one we supply, and pressure-tests hot and cold lines within handyman plumbing scope.

Kitchen and bath faucet scopes

  • Deck-mount and single-hole lavatory swaps
  • Kitchen pull-down and side-spray replacements
  • Supply line upgrades when braided lines are stiff or corroded
  • Escutcheon and gasket corrections on tile and stone tops
  • Disposal and trap reconnection when kitchen bowls change depth

What we check before declaring success

We run both valves, look for weeps at cartridges and hose connections, and confirm drains still align with tailpieces. If shutoffs fail during the swap, we quote shutoff valve repair before forcing old valves.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Local faucet replacement FAQ — written for Pinellas County homeowners, not a national template.

Can a handyman replace a kitchen faucet?

Yes when connecting to existing shutoffs and sink openings within handyman plumbing scope.

Do I need to buy the faucet before you come?

You may supply your own fixture or ask us to quote supply — specify when booking.

What if my shutoff valves fail during replacement?

We quote shutoff replacement before proceeding — forcing old valves often causes bigger leaks.

Do you haul away the old faucet?

Old fixtures are typically removed from the work area; disposal details can be noted in your estimate.

Can you replace a faucet on granite or quartz?

Yes — we use appropriate mounting hardware and protect stone surfaces during swap.

How long does faucet replacement take?

Many standard swaps finish in a single visit when shutoffs and connections cooperate.
