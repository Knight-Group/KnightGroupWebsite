How We Work

Step 1: Free Estimate — Call or email us to schedule an appointment. Vincent will assess the work and provide a no-obligation estimate.
Step 2: Contract — We draft a contract detailing the scope of work, timeline, and cost so everything is clear upfront.
Step 3: Work Begins — After approval, work starts. Payments are split: a deposit at the start, remainder upon completion. We accept PayPal, Venmo, and major credit cards.
Step 4: Final Walkthrough — We walk you through the completed work and ensure you're satisfied before final payment.
