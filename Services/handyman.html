Handyman Services You Can Trust

With over 15 years of experience in home repair and remodeling, Knight Group Handyman Services offers expert solutions for all your home improvement needs. Our primary handyman, Vincent Knight, brings a wealth of experience in plumbing, carpentry, drywall, electrical work, and more. Whether it’s a minor repair or a complete remodel, we have the expertise to get the job done right.

Vincent Knight - Expert Handyman

Vincent Knight has over 10 years of experience as a journeyman plumber, working for Roto Rooter in Pittsburgh, PA. During his time there, he handled everything from commercial building maintenance to sewer line repairs, camera inspections, and large-scale excavation work. He has literally done it all—plumbing repairs, sewer systems, water heater installations, and more. In addition, he has over 15 years of experience in home repair and remodeling, managing properties throughout Pinellas County as part of our family’s property management business. He specializes in bathroom remodels, plumbing, and general home renovations, making him your go-to expert for any handyman project.

Bathroom Remodels: Full bathroom renovations including plumbing, tiling, fixture installations, and more.

How We Work

At Knight Group Handyman Services, we follow a straightforward process to ensure your satisfaction:

  • Step 1: Call or email us to schedule an appointment. Vincent will come to your property to assess the work needed and provide a free, no-obligation estimate.
  • Step 2: We draft a contract detailing the scope of the work, estimated timeline, and cost.
  • Step 3: After approval, work begins! Payments are typically split into a deposit at the start and the remainder upon completion. We accept PayPal, Venmo, and most major credit cards.
  • Step 4: Upon completion, we walk you through the work done and ensure you're satisfied with the results before final payment is made.
