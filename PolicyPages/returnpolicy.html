Our Commitment to Quality

Every job Knight Group completes is backed by our workmanship guarantee. We're registered, insured, and proud of the quality we deliver — but if anything doesn't hold up, we come back and make it right.

30-Day Workmanship Guarantee

If any work we've completed shows signs of a workmanship defect within 30 days of completion, we will return and correct it at no charge. No hassle, no argument — we stand behind what we build.

What the Guarantee Covers

Our workmanship guarantee applies to all labor and installation performed by Knight Group technicians, including:

  • Plumbing fixture installations and repairs
  • Carpentry, shelving, and framing work
  • Drywall patching and finishing
  • Interior and exterior painting
  • Door and window installation
  • Electrical fixture work
  • General repairs and punch-list items

What the Guarantee Does Not Cover

  • Damage caused by customer modifications after completion
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Issues arising from pre-existing structural conditions beyond our scope
  • Material defects (covered under manufacturer warranty where applicable)

How to Request a Callback

If you have a concern about completed work, contact us within 30 days and we'll schedule a no-cost return visit to assess and correct the issue.

  1. Call or text us at (813) 649-3341
  2. Describe the issue and when the original work was completed
  3. We'll confirm a callback appointment — usually within 48 hours

Refund Policy

In cases where a workmanship issue cannot be corrected to a reasonable standard, Knight Group will discuss partial or full refund options depending on the circumstances. Refunds are a last resort — our goal is always to fix the problem and leave you satisfied.

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of job completion
  • Materials purchased and installed are non-refundable unless defective
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days
