Payment Policy

Clear and transparent payment terms for all our handyman services.

Effective Date: December 15, 2024

1. Accepted Payment Methods

Knight Group accepts various payment methods to make your transactions convenient and secure:

Primary Payment Methods

  • Cash: Accepted for all services
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover
  • Digital Payments: PayPal, Venmo, and other approved digital payment platforms
  • Bank Transfers: Wire transfers and ACH payments for larger projects

2. Payment Terms

Small Projects (Under $500)

  • Payment due upon completion of work
  • No deposit required for most small projects
  • Payment accepted immediately after final inspection

Medium Projects ($500 - $2,000)

  • 25% deposit may be required to secure scheduling
  • Remaining balance due upon project completion
  • Materials costs may require upfront payment

Large Projects (Over $2,000)

  • 30-50% deposit required to begin work
  • Progress payments may be scheduled for extended projects
  • Final payment due within 5 business days of completion
  • Detailed payment schedule provided in written estimate

3. Deposit and Down Payment Policy

  • Deposits: Secure your project slot and cover initial material costs
  • Refundable Conditions: Deposits are refundable if we cannot complete the work as agreed
  • Non-refundable Conditions: Deposits are non-refundable if customer cancels after materials are ordered or work has begun
  • Material Deposits: Required for custom orders or specialty materials

4. Emergency Service Billing

  • Emergency Service Fee: $150 minimum for urgent calls outside normal business hours
  • Emergency Payment: Due immediately upon service completion
  • Service Scope: Emergency services focus on damage prevention and temporary solutions
  • Follow-up Work: Permanent repairs scheduled separately under standard terms

5. Late Payment Policy

  • Grace Period: 10 business days from invoice date
  • Late Fees: 1.5% per month on overdue balances after grace period
  • Collection: Accounts over 60 days may be sent to collection
  • Work Suspension: Additional work may be suspended for accounts over 30 days past due

6. Estimates and Pricing

  • Free Estimates: Provided for most projects within our service area
  • Written Quotes: Detailed estimates provided before work begins
  • Price Validity: Estimates valid for 30 days from issue date
  • Change Orders: Additional work requires written approval and may affect payment terms

7. Materials and Supplies

  • Customer Supplied: Customers provide all paints, materials, and supplies unless otherwise agreed
  • Knight Group Purchased: When we purchase materials, payment is due before or upon delivery
  • Material Markup: Industry-standard markup applied to materials we purchase
  • Returns: Unused materials may be returned at customer's expense

8. Warranty and Guarantee Payment Terms

  • Warranty Work: Covered repairs performed at no charge during warranty period
  • Warranty Payment: No payment required for legitimate warranty claims
  • Non-warranty Issues: Issues outside warranty scope billed under standard terms
  • Guarantee Period: Varies by project type, detailed in service agreement

9. Financing Options

  • Payment Plans: Available for qualified customers on projects over $1,000
  • Extended Terms: Case-by-case basis with approved credit
  • Senior Discounts: Available for customers 65+ on select services
  • Military Discounts: Active duty and veteran discounts available

10. Disputed Charges

  • Resolution Process: Contact us within 10 business days of invoice
  • Documentation: Provide specific details about disputed items
  • Investigation: We will investigate all legitimate disputes promptly
  • Good Faith: Payment not required for legitimately disputed work during resolution
