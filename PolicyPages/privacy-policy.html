Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10/20/2024

At Knight Group, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [www.knightgroup.com] (the “Site”) or use our services. By using the Site or our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide to us when using our website or contacting us through our Contact Us form. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Information provided in the message or description field in the Contact Us form

We may also collect non-personal information about your device, browser, and how you interact with the Site using Google Analytics.

Information Automatically Collected

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic, user behavior, and Site performance. Google Analytics collects information such as IP addresses, browser types, device information, and user interaction data (e.g., time spent on pages, pages visited). For more information on how Google collects and processes this data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and messages submitted through the Contact Us form.
  • To provide you with updates or information related to our services.
  • To analyze website traffic and improve the performance and usability of our Site (via Google Analytics).
  • To list your inquiry or product information on free listings for promotional purposes.
  • To process orders for our services (such as Handyman Services, Programming & Database Services, and handcrafted wooden furniture).

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We currently do not use cookies for tracking purposes, but our use of Google Analytics may include the collection of data through third-party cookies. Google Analytics uses cookies to track users' sessions and interactions with the website. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with service providers such as Google (via Google Analytics) for the purpose of analyzing website performance.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by governmental authorities.

5. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites such as social media platforms and business pages (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, GitHub). Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party website they visit.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you contact us through the Site, we will keep your information on file to process your request and provide follow-up communication. If you would like your data to be deleted, please contact us at nknight@knightgroup.com, and we will take reasonable steps to delete your information.

7. User Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information from our records. We will honor such requests unless we are required by law to retain your information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at nknight@knightgroup.com.

8. Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 18. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information from our records.

10. International Users

Although we primarily offer Handyman Services in Pinellas County, Florida, our Programming & Database Services are offered remotely and may be accessed by international clients. If you are accessing our Site from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred, processed, and stored in the United States, where privacy laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Effective Date.” We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
