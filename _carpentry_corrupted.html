Frequently Asked Questions

What carpentry services do you offer?

We offer custom shelving, cabinet installation, trim and molding, door frame repair, deck repairs, wood rot replacement, and general framing work throughout Pinellas County and Safety Harbor.

Can you build custom built-in shelves or storage?

Yes — custom built-in bookshelves, closet systems, and storage solutions are a specialty. We design and build to fit your exact space.

Do you repair rotted wood or fascia?

Yes. Wood rot is common in Florida's humidity. We identify and replace rotted boards, fascia, trim, and structural wood as needed.

Can you frame a new wall or opening?

We handle non-load-bearing wall framing, doorway openings, and closet build-outs throughout Pinellas County. Load-bearing work is assessed and referred to a licensed contractor if needed.
