When should I use contact instead of booking?

Use contact for service-area checks, schedule questions, photo follow-ups, and lighter first messages. Use booking when you already know the scope and want to send the address, timing, and full project details in one estimate request.

Does Knight Group serve all of Pinellas County?

Yes. We serve Safety Harbor, Clearwater, Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Largo, Oldsmar, Seminole, St. Petersburg, and nearby Pinellas County communities.

How fast will you respond?

Standard contact messages are answered within 24 hours on business days. For urgent leaks, security issues, or active damage, call or text (813) 649-3341.

Can I text photos with my question?

Yes. Text photos to (813) 649-3341 or mention in your message that you have photos to share. That helps confirm fit before scheduling a visit.
