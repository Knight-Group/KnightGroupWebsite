Service area questions

These are the most common routing questions homeowners ask before they request an estimate.

Do you serve Pinellas, Hillsborough, and Pasco County?

Yes. Knight Group is based in Safety Harbor and serves Pinellas County daily, plus selected Hillsborough communities (Tampa, Westchase, Carrollwood, Citrus Park, Town 'n' Country) and Pasco communities along our Gulf-coast route (Holiday, Trinity, New Port Richey, Elfers, and nearby areas). Send your address to confirm edge routes.

Which page should I use if I need a small-job carpenter or trim repair?

Start with Carpentry & Framing for trim, wood-rot repair, shelving, built-ins, framing corrections, and small-job carpenter work across Pinellas County.

Do painting and drywall jobs use the same estimate route?

Yes. Use Painting & Finishing for interior or exterior painting, cabinet refreshes, drywall prep, and texture matching, then request the estimate through the page or the main booking form.

How do I confirm whether my property is inside your current route?

Send your city, repair type, and preferred timing through booking or contact. Knight Group confirms whether your neighborhood fits our Tampa Bay route from Safety Harbor before scheduling — especially for borderline areas like Temple Terrace or Land O' Lakes.

Is Knight Group a handyman company that serves all of Pinellas County?

Yes. Knight Group Handyman Services LLC is based in Safety Harbor and regularly serves homeowners across Pinellas County. For city-specific routing, see the Clearwater handyman page or the broader Pinellas County handyman overview.
