## What Not To Do

- Do not create a batch of thin city pages before the current core set indexes.
- Do not point every citation or partner link to the homepage. Spread them across booking and the service pages above.
- Do not describe Knight Group as a general contractor or mix Knight Logics positioning into listings.
- Do not chase low-quality bulk directory spam ahead of chambers, major platforms, and real local partners.
