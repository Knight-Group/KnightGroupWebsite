Handyman Pricing in Pinellas County, FL

Clear hourly rates, no 2-hour minimums, and fast quotes for repairs, installations, punch-list work, and emergency calls across Pinellas County.

Transparent Rates for Local Handyman Work

At Knight Group, we price handyman work the way homeowners want it priced: clearly, upfront, and without padded minimums. Whether you need a short punch-list visit or a larger repair day, your estimate is built around the actual scope, prep time, and risk level of the work.

What Affects Your Final Price

Most estimates come down to four things: the type of work, how much prep is needed, whether special handling or higher-liability work is involved, and whether the visit is after-hours or an emergency call.

Typical pricing factors:

  • Simple repair versus multi-step installation work
  • Access issues, ladder work, or heavy-item handling
  • How much prep, teardown, or cleanup the job needs
  • Whether materials pickup, specialty hardware, or emergency timing are involved

  • Furniture assembly

  • Exterior fixture installs (flood lights, outdoor fans, etc.)

Note: For licensed electrical, plumbing, or HVAC work, we can provide temporary fixes and help coordinate with licensed professionals.

Why We're More Affordable Than Our Competitors

Many national handyman franchises charge $120–$150 per hour with mandatory 2-hour minimums, meaning you pay $240–$300 even for a simple 45-minute job. Worse yet, they often add hidden fees that aren't disclosed upfront:

Common Hidden Fees from Competitors:

Service call fees: $50-$100 just to show up
Transportation charges: $25-$75 extra for travel
Materials markup: 20-50% above retail cost
Diagnostic fees: Additional cost even for simple assessments
Real Example: 45-Minute Outlet Installation
✅ Knight Group: $150 total (no hidden fees)
❌ Typical Competitor: $240 (2-hr min) + $75 service fee + $25 travel = $340+

At Knight Group, our pricing is 100% transparent. What we quote is what you pay—no surprise charges, no service fees, no travel costs. For larger jobs, our hourly rates ($75-$100) remain significantly lower than most competitors, and we never require minimums that inflate your bill.

Materials & Parts

You're only billed for the time spent — so if we need to run and pick up materials, it's included in the hourly rate. If you'd like to have all materials ready, we'll gladly work with what you provide.

Materials Markup: We charge a modest 10% markup on materials to cover purchasing, handling, and warranty responsibility. This is significantly lower than most competitors who charge 20-50% markup.

Where We Quote Work Most Often

We regularly quote handyman work in Safety Harbor, Clearwater, Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Largo, Pinellas Park, and St. Petersburg. Short repair lists, fixture swaps, drywall patches, and small install jobs are often the best-value calls because we can bundle multiple items into one visit.

If you are not sure whether your project is a standard rate job, a specialty job, or an emergency call, send the details and we will break that down before work starts.
