Vincent Knight - Owner & Expert Handyman

Vincent Knight brings over a decade of experience as a journeyman plumber, having worked with Roto Rooter in Pittsburgh, PA. His extensive expertise includes commercial building maintenance, sewer line repairs, camera inspections, and large-scale excavation projects. From plumbing repairs and water heater installations to complete sewer system overhauls - he's done it all.

Beyond plumbing, Vincent has over 15 years of experience in home repair and remodeling, managing properties across Pinellas County through our family's property management business. His work includes drywall repair, trim and finish work, fixture replacement, window and door repairs, flooring, and renovation punch lists. Whether it's a one-off repair or a longer home-improvement project, Vincent brings the same direct, hands-on approach to every job.

How We Work

Step 1: Free Estimate — Call or email us to schedule an appointment. Vincent will assess the work and provide a no-obligation estimate.
Step 2: Contract — We draft a contract detailing the scope of work, timeline, and cost so everything is clear upfront.
Step 3: Work Begins — After approval, work starts. Payments are split: a deposit at the start, remainder upon completion. We accept PayPal, Venmo, and major credit cards.
Step 4: Final Walkthrough — We walk you through the completed work and ensure you're satisfied before final payment.
General Pricing — Standard jobs start at $150 for the first hour. Additional hours billed at $75/hr. See our full pricing page for specialty rates.

Frequently Asked Questions

What handyman services does Knight Group offer in Safety Harbor?

Knight Group provides plumbing repairs, drywall, painting, carpentry, electrical fixture work, door and window installation, general home repairs, and emergency services throughout Safety Harbor and all of Pinellas County.

How much does a handyman cost in Pinellas County, FL?

Knight Group's handyman rates range from $75–$150/hour depending on the job type. There is no 2-hour minimum — you pay only for actual time worked. Free written estimates are available for all projects.

Is Knight Group licensed and insured in Florida?

Yes. Knight Group Handyman Services LLC is registered and fully insured in Florida, giving homeowners, landlords, and property managers peace of mind on every job.

Do you take small handyman jobs?

Absolutely. We have no minimum job size. Whether it's a leaky faucet, a single shelf install, or a full room repair, we handle jobs of any size throughout Pinellas County.
