Our Story & Mission

Knight Group Handyman Services LLC has been proudly serving Pinellas County for over 15 years, building a reputation for quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service. What started as a small local business has grown into a trusted name in home improvement and maintenance.

Our mission is simple: to provide homeowners and businesses with professional, affordable, and reliable handyman services that enhance the value and comfort of their properties. We believe every project, no matter how big or small, deserves the same level of attention to detail and commitment to excellence.

From basic repairs and maintenance to complete home renovations, we handle every job with the professionalism and care it deserves. Our registered and insured team brings years of experience and a commitment to using quality materials and proven techniques.
