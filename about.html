Our story

Built on consistency, craft, and local trust

From everyday repairs to room refreshes, Knight Group focuses on clear communication, honest pricing, and work that holds up in real Florida homes.

Knight Group Handyman Services LLC brings together two practical backgrounds that Florida homeowners notice quickly: 15 years as a journeyman plumber and 10 years in property management across the state. That combination means fewer surprises on plumbing-related repairs, rental turnovers, punch-list work, and the maintenance issues that show up in real occupied homes.

Our mission is simple: provide homeowners and property owners with professional, affordable, and reliable handyman services that protect the value and comfort of their properties. Every project no matter how big or small deserves clear communication, honest pricing, and work done the right way.

From fixture swaps and drywall repairs to room refreshes and renovation support, we handle jobs with the professionalism of someone who has worked in the trade and managed properties in Florida's climate.
